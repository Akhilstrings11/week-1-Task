MAHINDRA THAR PRICE & SPECIFICARIONS:-

The Mahindra Thar Price in Hyderabad starts from ₹ 12.24 Lakh and goes upto ₹ 20.60 Lakh. Thar is a SUV, offered with a choice of 1497 cc, 2184 cc Diesel and 1997 cc Petrol engine options. The Thar on road price in Hyderabad for 1497 cc Diesel engine ranges between ₹ 12.24 - 13.87 Lakh while 2184 cc Diesel engine ranges between ₹ 17.73 - 20.60 Lakh. For Petrol engine powered by 1997 cc on road price ranges between ₹ 16.85 - 19.77 Lakh.

Mahindra Thar is available in 8 Diesel variants, out of which 2 are automatic and 6 are manual. The base Diesel variant Thar AX Opt 4-Str Hard Top Diesel RWD starts at Rs. 9.99 Lakh. and the top-end Diesel variant Thar LX 4-Str Hard Top Diesel AT is priced at Rs. 16.29 Lakh.
